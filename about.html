ABOUT

01 — PHILOSOPHY

A visual storyteller exploring the boundaries between digital and film photography. My work captures the raw essence of moments — the textures of time, the weight of memory, and the quiet poetry found in everyday life.

Based in Brest, France, I draw inspiration from the landscapes, people, and stories that surround me. Each project is an experiment in seeing / finding beauty in the overlooked, meaning in the mundane.

02 — APPROACH

Whether shooting on film or digital, my approach remains the same: raw, intentional, and deeply personal. I believe every frame should breathe, carrying the atmosphere of the moment it was born in.
